Arcticons

A monotone line-based icon pack for Android.

With over 7000 icons, Arcticons is one of the largest free & open source icon-packs available. Featuring consistent and elegant handcrafted icons, giving you a minimalistic clutter-free experience on your phone.
Powered by a community of icon creators all around the world!
